Why The Netherlands?

Netherlands, The country of Canals and Culture

The Netherlands is celebrated for its high quality of life, robust social support, and progressive values. Its healthcare and education systems are publicly funded and accessible, ensuring comprehensive care and educational opportunities for all. Known for promoting work-life balance, the Netherlands provides ample vacation time and shorter working hours, supporting a well-rounded lifestyle. The country's infrastructure, including efficient public transport and extensive cycling paths, adds to its appeal and helps maintain a healthy environment.

Furthermore, the Dutch are recognized for their openness and inclusivity, creating a multicultural society where people from various backgrounds feel at home. Major cities like Amsterdam and Rotterdam offer rich cultural experiences, with world-class museums, historic sites, and a thriving economy that fosters innovation in technology and sustainability. With a strong commitment to environmental protection, the Netherlands is an attractive place to live, work, and visit.

My favorite cafés in the Netherlands

Koffie & Zo

Koffie & Zo in Delft is a cozy café known for its unique vibe, doubling as a vintage store where you can buy decor items. Open since 2015, it offers quality coffee, homemade treats, and vegan-friendly options in a charming setting.

Address:

Peperstraat 17, 2611 CH Delft

What I like about it

Koffie & Zo has a cozy, inviting atmosphere with a vintage twist, where each decor item doubles as something you can buy, adding to its unique charm. Their quality coffee and homemade treats, including vegan and gluten-free options, make it a warm and inclusive spot. It’s a delightful blend of café and boutique, perfect for a relaxed day in Delft.

Back to Black

Back to Black is a popular Amsterdam café and roastery, known for its cozy atmosphere, high-quality coffee, and homemade pastries. Established in 2014, the café roasts its own beans and emphasizes sustainability.

Address:

Weteringstraat 48, 1017 SP Amsterdam

What I like about it

I appreciate Back to Black for its dedication to high-quality, freshly roasted coffee and its cozy, welcoming vibe that feels authentically local. The homemade pastries and vegan-friendly options add a thoughtful touch, making it inclusive and satisfying for various tastes.

MAMS coffee & more Rotterdam

MAMS Coffee & More in Rotterdam is known for its quality coffee, homemade cakes, and vegan-friendly options. Located near the cultural Jules Deelderplein, it offers a spacious setting with a large terrace and supports sustainability through initiatives like Too Good To Go to reduce food waste.

Address:

Van Speykstraat 192, 3014 VS Rotterdam

What I like about it

I love MAMS for its quality coffee, spacious terrace, and commitment to sustainability. Its location near Jules Deelderplein adds a unique cultural vibe, making it a great spot to enjoy Rotterdam.
